///////////////////////////////
Controls
///////////////////////////////

tab: change selected cube

w, a, s, d, e, f: move camera

i, j, k, l, u, o: change selected cube velocity (also works with number pad arrow keys and PGUP and PGDOWN)

space: stop selected cube

c: change camera to follow selected cube

v: change camera to follow ship

i, j, k, l: control ship when selected (also works with number pad arrow keys and PGUP and PGDOWN)

q: quit
